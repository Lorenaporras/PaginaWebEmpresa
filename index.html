Why this is different

Spanish adapted to
your real life

Learning Spanish shouldn't feel like following a script where you cannot truly be yourself because of the language barrier.

My classes are designed for English speakers who want to communicate with more confidence, sound more natural, and participate in real conversations without feeling blocked or limited.

This is not a rigid method or a generic program. Every learning process is designed according to your goals, personality, difficulties, and the real-life situations where you need to use Spanish.

The goal is not memorizing grammar rules without context. The goal is helping you understand how the language works so you can use Spanish more naturally and consciously — sounding like the adult you already are.

Sessions may focus on conversation, fluency, pronunciation, listening comprehension, naturalness, cultural integration, or grammar applied to real communication.

Meet your teacher

Hi, I'm Lorena

I'm a professional Spanish teacher specialized in Spanish as a Foreign Language, with more than five years of experience teaching adult English speakers.

My approach combines real conversation, cognitive grammar (focused on the logic of the language), language coaching, pronunciation, and personalized support — all focused on helping you use Spanish more naturally and consciously.

I've mainly worked with students who understand a lot but struggle to speak, feel insecure communicating, mentally translate everything, or want to sound more authentic and spontaneous in real-life situations.
